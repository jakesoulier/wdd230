This town has the name it has for a reason. This is the snake river that passes the Church of Jesus Christ temple and has water falls right in the middle of downtown.

Downtown Idaho Falls is fun of cool stores and shops all along the same street like you can see here.

Here stands the Museum of Idaho. It offers a great exhibit and changes the theme regularly. It just closed down the Genghis Khan exhibit and we are waiting on the announcement for the next exhibit.

This Idaho Fall sign is the same logo that you can find in many different places scattered across the town.

Coming up to Idaho Falls from the south? Take this road exit as you enter this wonderfull place.

This is a great activity to explore as there are many different animals that can be found here.

The Auditorium District hosts different plays and shows that are very enjoyable.

This giant fountain stands tall to represent the history of Idaho Falls. The city used to be called Eagle Rock back in the late 1800's.
